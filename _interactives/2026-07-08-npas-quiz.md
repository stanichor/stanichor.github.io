This quiz uses the 26-item [Nerdy Personality Attributes Scale](https://openpsychometrics.org/tests/NPAS/development/) (NPAS) to estimate how nerdy you are and match you to one of three nerd types: Hobbyist, Geek, or Scholar.

Answer each item, and at the end you’ll get a Nerd Score from 0–100, plus a profile of your type.
